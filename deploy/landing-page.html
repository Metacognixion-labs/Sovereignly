The Problem

Serverless shouldn't mean surrendering control.

Vendor lock-in is the hidden tax. Proprietary runtimes, cold-start penalties, and data you can't export — the bill comes due when you try to leave.

🔒
Proprietary runtimes
Your functions only run on their infra. Migrating means rewriting from scratch.
💸
Pricing held hostage
Cold starts, egress fees, concurrency limits. The model only makes sense until you scale.
🚧
No local parity
Cloud functions behave differently in prod. You find out at the worst possible moment.
